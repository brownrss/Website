Who can join?

Anyone who takes a class online can join. Our pilot program is targeted towards students at Brown University, but we hope to expand in the near future so that students studying online can meet others across the country and even the world.

What's the point?

Learning online doesn't mean that you have to be alone. We want to create a social ecosystem for students studying remotely so that they can share resources, keep each other motivated, and expand their network.

Are there any perks?

Definitely! Once a month we host an event and give out prizes like Amazon Gift Cards, Remote Study Group Swag, and more!

Why should I join?

Because you have everything to gain and nothing to lose. As the COVID-19 pandemic continues we will still face periods of isolation. Students will still be asked to quarantine and many others will continue choosing to study remotely. However, socializing is part of the college experience. By joining, you have access to a network filled with people just like you who you can lean on for support.

When do you meet?

Sunday - Friday from 12 pm - 2 pm Eastern Standard Time. However, by clicking on the interactive map you can find the schedule of other students in your area who are available to study. Feel free to reach out to them to set up a date and time to study. Very soon we will also offer paid remote moderator positions where you can staff a remote study room and get paid based on how many people join!
